# FruitShoot User Documentation

## Getting Started

When opening the FruitShoot app, you will be prompted with a login page.

- If you already have an account:
  - Enter your username and password
  - Select **Get Started**

- If you do not have an account:
  - Select **Create an account**
  - Enter your email, username, and password
  - Submit to register

---

## Uploading a Fruit Image

From the home page, select **Upload Picture**.

### Option 1: Upload from Library
- Select **Library**
- Choose an image from your phone’s photo library

### Option 2: Take a New Photo
- Select **Camera**
- Take a new picture using your device

### Add Description (Optional)
- Tap the **Description** textbox
- Enter any relevant details about the image

### Upload
- Tap **Upload**
- The app will:
  - Analyze the fruit
  - Determine ripeness
  - Navigate to the results page
  - Recommend recipes based on the fruit

---

## Exploring New Recipes

- Navigate to the **Explore** tab from the home screen
- Browse recipes shared by other users

### Actions
- **Save a recipe**: Tap the **Save** button on a recipe card
- **View details**: Tap the recipe card

---

## Creating a Recipe

- Go to the **Profile** tab
- Select **Create Recipe**

### Enter:
- Title
- Ingredients
- Steps

### Submit
- Tap **Submit**
- You will be returned to your profile with a confirmation

---

## Importing a Recipe

- Go to the **Profile** tab
- Select **Import Recipe**

### Steps:
- Paste a link to a recipe webpage
- Tap **Import**
- Fields will autofill automatically

### Finalize:
- Review the information
- Tap **Submit** to upload

---

## Editing Profile Picture

- Go to the **Profile** tab
- Tap your current profile picture
- Select a new image from your library
- Tap **Choose**

---

## Editing Profile Allergies

- Go to the **Profile** tab
- Tap **Add**
- Select an allergy from the dropdown menu

---

## Viewing Images

- Go to the **Profile** tab
- Tap **My Images**
- Browse all uploaded images

---

## Viewing Recipes

- Go to the **Profile** tab

### Options:
- **Saved Recipes**: View recipes you saved
- **My Recipes**: View recipes you created
